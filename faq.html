Product Questions

Is teeth whitening safe? +

Yes, our product has been FDA approved since 1983 with over 40 years of proven safety. Available in pharmacies across America, it safely whitens teeth without weakening them.

How long before I see results? +

Individual results vary, but most users notice whiter teeth within 1-2 weeks of use. For optimal results, we recommend following the recommended usage method for at least 2 weeks. Results vary from person to person.

Will I experience tooth sensitivity? +

Our product contains sensitivity-relief complex, making it comfortable for most users. If you experience strong pain or discomfort, discontinue use and contact our customer support.

What are the ingredients? +

Our product features high-concentration whitening agents, enamel-strengthening formula, and sensitivity-relief complex in a dentist-developed professional grade formula. 100% cruelty-free manufacturing.

How do I use the product? +

Use once weekly: dry teeth, apply 1ml gel per tooth, LED light for 6-8 minutes, repeat 3-6 times. Continue for 4 weeks for optimal results.

What stains does it remove? +

Effective against coffee, tea, red wine, tobacco stains, and age-related yellowing. Not effective on medication-induced discoloration or artificial teeth (crowns, fillings, etc.).

How long do results last? +

Duration varies with lifestyle habits, typically 6 months to 1 year. Avoiding staining foods and beverages helps maintain results longer.

Can pregnant or nursing women use this? +

We recommend pregnant and nursing women avoid use. Also not recommended for those under 18 years of age.
