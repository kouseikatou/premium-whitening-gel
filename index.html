HOW TO USE

Professional Whitening in 4 Simple Steps

01. PREPARE

Dry teeth with cotton
Apply vaseline to lips

02. APPLY GEL

1ml gel per tooth
Avoid gums

03. ACTIVATE

LED light 6-8 minutes
Repeat 3-6 times

04. REPEAT

Once weekly
For 4 weeks

Additional Tips for Success

Pre-Treatment Preparation

  • Avoid strong brushing 8 hours before treatment
  • Avoid eating/drinking immediately before
  • Approach in relaxed state

Post-Treatment Care

  • No eating/drinking for 30 minutes
  • Avoid staining foods for 24 hours
  • Regular brushing is fine

Stop Usage When

  • Strong tooth sensitivity occurs
  • Gum pain or discomfort exists
  • During pregnancy or breastfeeding
